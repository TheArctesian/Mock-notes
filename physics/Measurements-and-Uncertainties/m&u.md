|-----|---|---|
|  Time | s  | second  |
|  length   |  meter | m |

### Absolute uncertainty
Absolute uncertainty is the uncertainty in a measurement, represented in the relevant SI units. 
```
for a stopwatch the minium number is 0.01 therefore the uncertainty will be x ± 0.01. 
```
